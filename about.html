ABOUT


HEY, I'M TERRON

I'm a photographer based in the Bellevue Washington area. I specialize in wedding photography, but I always enjoy an opportunity to step out of the box with fresh, new and adventurous ideas. I would describe my signature photographing style as clean, timeless, and modern. Most of my clients are based in the city of Seattle, Bellevue and the outlying communities.
